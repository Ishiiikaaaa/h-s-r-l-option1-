About Our Company

Who We Are

Highway Stops Retail Limited was launched in December 2011 by Chief Executive Officer Sharad Raja. Since its foundation, the company has focused on delivering premium fuel brands and exceptional retail experiences across the UK.

Our Vision

To become one of the most efficient and profitable independent forecourt operators in the UK by combining premium fuel brands with outstanding customer service.

Our Growth

Backed by HSBC investors, HSRL continues to expand strategically across the South East, operating 14 stations under BP, Esso and Shell brands.
