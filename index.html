Welcome to Highway Stops Retail

On the 22nd December 2020, one of the UK's leading independent forecourt operators, Motor Fuel Group (MFG), was sold and Highway Stops Retail Limited was launched by chief executive officer, Sharad Raja who originally jointly owned MFG. Sharad is joined by his son Shilan Raja, along with Tony Head and Sellarajah Gunalan as development director. All three directors have considerable experience of the forecourt sector.
